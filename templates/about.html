About
This website is a simple youtube's channel analyzer using its statistic that retrieved by the API key from google cloud platform. The statistic will be divided into two categories, the first one is '1' which means higher by the average of the statistic, and the second one is '0' which means lower by the average of the statistic. After that, the statistic will be classified by its segmentation so the website can grade the channel wether it is good or not.
